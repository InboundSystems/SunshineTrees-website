What We Do

Our Services

From a quick trim to full removal — handled with care, the right gear, and a proper clean-up.

🪚

Tree Lopping

Precision pruning and lopping to reshape, reduce, and maintain your trees. Keeps your yard clean and your trees healthier for longer.

🌳

Tree Removal

Safe, controlled removal of trees up to 10 metres. We clear all debris and leave your property neat, tidy, and hazard-free.

⚙️

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal below ground level. Reclaim your lawn, eliminate trip hazards, and stop regrowth for good.

🌴

Palm Cleaning

Expert frond skirt removal and palm maintenance. Keeps your palms looking sharp, prevents fire hazards, and stops pests nesting.

✂️

Hedge Trimming

Crisp, clean lines for hedges and shrubs of all sizes. We work with your shape in mind and leave no mess behind.

🚜

Land Clearing

Clearing blocks and properties for development, landscaping, or renovation. Efficient, thorough, and priced fairly.
